Adapt or Die

In this game, creatures evolve to survive. Click on them to view their mutation details (desktop only). Their speed, size, and mutation rate affect their survival.

Each round ends with creatures in the safe zone reproducing. You can drag the safe zone to influence their evolution. For instance, positioning it in the middle encourages creatures to move there.

A creature that reproduces will either create more of itself or a new mutation of itself, based on its mutation probability.
